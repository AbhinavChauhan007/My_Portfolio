DIT UNIVERSITY

DIT University, erstwhile Dehradun Institute of Technology, is a university in Dehradun(India), established by the non-profit Unison Group. DIT University has been established by Govt. of Uttarakhand vide Act No.10 of 2013 dated 15 February 2013 and is recognised by the UGC under section 2 of the UGC Act, 1956.

DIT University

The University is part of the Association of Indian Universities (AIU). It has been recognised by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), Council of Architecture (COA) and Pharmacy Council of India (PCI). It is an ISO 21001:2018 certified University.

DIT University is the first University in Uttarakhand to receive QS I-Gauge E-Lead, a certificate for exceptional e-learning methods such as academic digitisation.

Placements: More than 80% students get placed every year all over working experience is good. Students are provided internships and PPO also. The placement and training department are always active. Teacher are supportive and help students to come out with flying colors. University the highest package was from Amazon.

Infrastructure: The infrastructure is good. All the classes are echo proof well-ventilated corridors. There is an individual building for Cafeteria and CCD. Hostels have single double and triple sharing with a good mess. Food Menu is different every day Hostel provides variety in food, University have basketball and volleyball court.

Faculty: Teachers are very helpful and knowledgeable. Most of the teachers are PhD holder and are very helpful as they inspire you and also happy to guide us and work on our projects. We have different entrepreneur cell which helps us to work on our idea and make it come true.

Excellent Academic Ambience : A 21-acre oasis situated in the pristine Doon Valley overlooking the Mussoorie foothills. A home away from home, amid scenic beauty.

Entrepreneurship Development : Our revolutionary in-house Entrepreneurship Development Cell focuses on innovations across diverse fields, helping students with their start-up ideas.

Holistic Education : An environment that encourages self-learning, sports, social, culture, technical skills and all-round development.
